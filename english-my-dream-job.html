英语作文

My Dream Job - 我梦想的职业

英语范文 2025-04-20

作文题目

题目：Write a composition of about 80-100 words on the topic "My Dream Job".

要求：1. What is your dream job? 2. Why do you want to do it? 3. What will you do to achieve your dream?

满分范文

My Dream Job

Everyone has a dream, and mine is to become a programmer. Ever since I wrote my first "Hello World" at the age of twelve, I have been fascinated by the magic of turning ideas into code.

There are two reasons why I choose this path. First, programming gives me the power to create. With just a computer and some lines of code, I can build a website, design a game, or even develop an app that helps people. Second, the world is becoming more digital, and programmers will play an increasingly important role in the future.

To achieve my dream, I am working hard in several ways. I practice coding on platforms like Luogu every day, and I have won prizes in programming competitions. Besides, I study math and English carefully, because they are both essential for programming. I believe that as long as I keep trying, my dream will come true.

中文翻译

每个人都有梦想，我的梦想是成为一名程序员。自从十二岁写出第一个"Hello World"以来，我就被将想法变成代码的魔力深深吸引。

我选择这条路有两个原因。首先，编程赋予我创造的力量。只需一台电脑和几行代码，我就能搭建网站、设计游戏，甚至开发帮助人们的应用程序。其次，世界正在变得更加数字化，程序员将在未来扮演越来越重要的角色。

为了实现我的梦想，我正在多方面努力。我每天在洛谷等平台上练习编程，并在编程竞赛中获得过奖项。此外，我认真学习数学和英语，因为它们对编程来说都是必不可少的。我相信，只要我坚持不懈，我的梦想终会实现。

高分亮点分析

亮点一：结构清晰 (Structure)

文章采用经典的三段式结构：

  • 开头段：点明主题（梦想是什么）
  • 主体段：阐述原因（为什么选择这个职业）
  • 结尾段：行动计划（如何实现梦想）

这种结构让阅卷老师一眼就能看到你的逻辑，是拿高分的基础。

亮点二：高级词汇 (Advanced Vocabulary)

普通表达高级替换效果
makecreate更有表现力
more and moreincreasingly更正式
very importantessential词汇更丰富
like it very muchbe fascinated by更地道
work hardkeep trying表达更积极

亮点三：过渡词 (Transition Words)

文章中使用了恰当的过渡词，使段落之间衔接自然：

  • Ever since...（自从……起）—— 引出背景
  • There are two reasons...（有两个原因）—— 引出主体
  • First... Second...（首先……其次……）—— 分点论述
  • Besides...（此外）—— 补充说明
  • I believe that...（我相信）—— 总结升华

亮点四：句型多样 (Sentence Variety)

  • 定语从句："I wrote my first 'Hello World' at the age of twelve"（省略了 that/which 的定语从句）
  • With 复合结构："With just a computer and some lines of code, I can build..."（介词短语作状语）
  • Not only... but also 思想：两段式的并列论述结构
  • as long as 条件句："as long as I keep trying, my dream will come true"（主将从现）
